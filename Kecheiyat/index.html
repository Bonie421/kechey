About

Learn more about our group

Nandi Catholic singing group

Our group as Kecheiyatab Nandi Catholic Chorale has given itself a mandate to revive the songs that had been nearly lost from our Nandi language, these songs were created by our people but since they had been displaced our group with the help of our Patron Fr. Hillary Nyongi was formed in order to record these lost and forgotten Nandi songs.
